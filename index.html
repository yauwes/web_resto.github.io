Our Story

Founded in 2010, Warung Bali was born from a passion to share authentic Balinese cuisine with the world. Our founder, I Made Wijaya, grew up in a small village in Ubud, learning traditional recipes from his grandmother.

Every dish we serve tells a story of Bali's rich culinary heritage, using only the freshest local ingredients and traditional cooking methods that have been passed down through generations.

Our restaurant is more than just a place to eat - it's a cultural experience that transports you to the heart of Bali with every bite.
